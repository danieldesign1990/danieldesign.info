About me

I graduated from the State Petroleum University in Ufa. While pursuing my primary studies, I simultaneously made mods for games, learned programming, and did basic web development by creating landing pages for clients. At some point, I realized that I enjoyed design the most, and after graduating from university, I started working as a freelance designer.

After three years of freelancing, I realized I wanted to grow in my field, so I moved to Moscow and started working on my first product. I wanted to develop my skills and learn how to improve life around me. At the beginning of 2022, I left Russia. Now, I live and work abroad.

For me, design is a system that must work seamlessly across devices and contexts while remaining unified. It should provide a first-class experience and address user needs and business objectives.
